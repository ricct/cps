Cloud Pilot Station (CPS) is a lightweight desktop app that lets you monitor and start/stop resources across Oracle Cloud Infrastructure and AWS — without switching consoles.

Why CPS

Unified Control

One panel to monitor and operate OCI Compute, AWS EC2, and (soon) DB services such as Autonomous DB / RDS.

Native & Fast

No heavy web shells. A native desktop experience built with .NET for smooth, responsive operations.

Search & Filter

Quickly find instances by name, tags, lifecycle state, or region.

How it Works

1

Connect

CPS detects ~/.oci/config and AWS profiles. Choose a profile and region.

2

Discover

CPS lists instances and DB services with status. Filter by state, tag, or name.

3

Operate

Start/stop or open details. Built-in confirmations keep operations safe.

Security & Privacy

CPS runs locally and uses your existing OCI/AWS credentials. No credentials are sent to third-party servers. Actions are executed via official CLIs/SDKs according to your profiles and policies.

FAQ

Does CPS change my cloud settings?
No. CPS only performs the operations you confirm (e.g., start/stop). It does not alter policies or network configs.
Which services are supported?
OCI Compute ,Base Databasse ,Autonomous DB.
AWS EC2 , RDS.
How do I switch regions or accounts?
Choose a profile/region at setting form. CPS reads your profiles and applies them per action.
